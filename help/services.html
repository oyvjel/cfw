Cumulus Firewall - Editing Services
A simple service is an IP protocol plus apropriate additional parameters such as port numbers, ICMP types etc.

Services may be a simple service, a service defined in the systems /etc/services file or a group of other services. The Services list displays all defined services except for the services in /etc/services. To edit a service you just click on the apropriate link in this list. See Editing a service

Note that groups are edited/created in a different form than all the other services.
